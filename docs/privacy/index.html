Privacy Policy

Shortform Auto Uploader · Last updated: 2026-07-20

Shortform Auto Uploader ("the Application") is a personal automation tool built and operated solely by its developer (GitHub: jayden-code-ai) to automatically publish short-form video content that the developer personally creates to the developer's own YouTube, Instagram, and TikTok accounts.

Scope

This Application is for single-user personal use only. It is not offered as a service to third parties, has no public sign-up, and does not collect, process, or store personal information belonging to anyone other than the developer operating it.

Data We Handle

  • OAuth access/refresh tokens issued by YouTube, Instagram, and TikTok — used exclusively to publish content to the developer's own connected accounts.
  • Video files and associated metadata (titles, descriptions, hashtags) created and owned by the developer.
  • Upload status logs (success/failure) kept locally for the developer's own monitoring.

Data Storage & Security

All tokens, video files, and logs are stored locally on the developer's own private hardware and are never transmitted to or shared with any third-party server, except for the direct API calls required to publish content to each platform on the developer's behalf.

No Third-Party Data Collection

This Application does not collect any information from end users, viewers, or any individual other than the developer operating the tool.

Data Retention & Deletion

The developer may delete stored tokens and logs at any time by removing the corresponding local files.
